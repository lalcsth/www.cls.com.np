Brand Identity

Brand identity is a strategic concept that encompasses the visual, emotional, and cultural elements that define a brand and distinguish it from others in the market. It goes beyond the tangible elements, such as logos and colors, and encompasses the brand's personality, values, and unique characteristics.

Advertising is a promotional activity that involves creating and disseminating messages or content to a target audience with the aim of influencing their behavior or perception towards a product, service, brand, or organization. It is a key component of marketing and communication strategies.
